Welcome!
About me:

South of France, born un the 90s, my favorite french airport is Nice LFMN, mostly Boeing.

I stream to share my hobby while having fun with you.
I play both on Xbox One and PC. My favorite are simuation, tycoon and sandbox but I play lots of different game. Here I will mainly play MSFS2020 or X-Plane but I could play something else, depending on my mood.

I always fly online, either VATSIM or IVAO for more immersion and realism as well as for its multiplayer.
Less overlay for more immersion.
My callsign is mainly from real flights, but I also use CARAVAiles, KVL, the Virtual Airline I fly with.
